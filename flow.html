Zevar.com Workflow 💎

Phase 1: Product Acquisition & Content 📸

This initial phase is all about sourcing our beautiful jewelry and preparing it for our customers, from vendor coordination to creating high-quality visuals.

  • **📸 Photography & Updates:** Get new product info and images from vendors or conduct in-house shoots.
  • **📁 Data Management:** Organize and store all product assets (photos, descriptions, SKUs).

Phase 2: Marketing & Customer Engagement 📢

Once products are ready, we focus on building our community. This phase covers updating our website, executing targeted social media campaigns, and maintaining a constant, helpful dialogue.

  • **💻 Website & Socials:** Update the website with new products and run targeted ad campaigns.
  • **🗣️ Customer Support:** Actively monitor and respond to inquiries on social media and support channels.
  • **💌 Newsletters:** Send regular email newsletters with new arrivals and special offers.

Phase 3: Order Processing & Fulfillment 📦

This is the core operational phase where sales are converted into happy customers. It involves managing orders through our CRM, coordinating with vendors, and automating shipping updates.

  • **🛒 Order Management:** All orders automatically sync to our central CRM system.
  • **🤝 Vendor Coordination:** The CRM notifies vendors of new orders and shipping deadlines.
  • **🚚 Shipping & Updates:** Automated tracking and notifications are sent to customers.
  • **🔄 Exception Management:** Handle returns, refunds, and cancellations smoothly through the CRM.

Phase 4: Continuous Improvement 🚀

Our work doesn't stop after a delivery. This final, ongoing phase is about learning and evolving. We regularly review our systems and explore new technologies to enhance the customer experience.

  • **📈 System Development:** Regularly review and update our CRM, website, and automation tools.
  • **💡 Innovation:** Continuously refine processes and explore new technologies to increase efficiency.
